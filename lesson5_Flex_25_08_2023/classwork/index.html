Dream Team

Long ago in a galaxy far far away... the Old Republic fell. On its ruins, the Sith Order created a galactic Empire, subjugating one planetary system after another. Alliance forces seek to overthrow the Dark Emperor and restore free rule to the galaxy. General Obi-Wan Kenobi returns after years of seclusion to once again face the Lord of Darkness Darth Vader. Together with him on the light side of the Force are the young pilot Luke, the son of Anakin Skywalker, Princess Senator Leia Organa, the legendary Correlian smuggler Han Solo and his friend Chewbacca.
